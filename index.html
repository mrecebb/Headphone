2015
Headphones are not just devices—they're a new lifestyle
In 2015, headphones evolved beyond mere tools for listening to music. They became a way to create personal space. Minimalist designs and the first active noise cancellation technologies marked the beginning of a new era.
2016
Where fashion meets technology
Headphones started being seen as fashion accessories. Colorful designs, foldable structures, and Bluetooth connectivity offered users both comfort and style.
2017
Cut the cords, focus on the sound
Wireless headphones took over the market. Apple AirPods and other brands ushered in a new era. Users embraced fewer restrictions and more freedom of movement.
2018
Headphones conquer the international market
In the era of technological globalization, headphones have become a remarkable phenomenon that has taken the international market by storm. The pleasure of listening to music, playing games or listening to audio content has driven the popularity of headphones to reach its peak. With innovative designs and advanced technology, modern headphones are not only a functional audio device, but also a highly desirable symbol of lifestyle.
2019
Hi-Fi technology becomes accessible to all
Premium sound quality was no longer reserved for audiophiles. With ANC (Active Noise Cancellation) and HD audio support, headphones delivered high-end experiences to everyday users.
2020
The rise of headphones in home offices
During COVID-19, headphones became essential tools for work and education. Models with microphones, long-term comfort, and video call optimization came to the forefront.
2021
Voice assistants now live in our ears
Siri, Google Assistant, and other voice assistants were integrated into headphones. Beyond music, users could now manage daily tasks through voice commands.
2022
Technology makes peace with nature
Headphone manufacturers shifted toward eco-conscious materials. Recyclable plastics, energy-efficient batteries, and ethical production processes became the new standard.
2023
The sound gateway to virtual worlds
With the rise of the Metaverse and VR technologies, headphones became central to immersive experiences. 3D audio and spatial sound transported users into entirely new realms.
2024
Sound tailored to your ears
AI-powered headphones began optimizing sound based on individual hearing profiles. A personalized audio experience became possible for everyone.
2025
Sound, style, and AI converge
Headphones are now an inseparable part of lifestyle. With wearable tech, biometric sensors, and real-time translation features, they synchronize not just with music—but with life itself.
